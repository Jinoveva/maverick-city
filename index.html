| WHY MAVERICK CITY MUSIC?

Maverick City Music: Redefining Worship and Breaking Records

Maverick City Music has revolutionised contemporary worship with their raw, spirit-filled sound, blending gospel, CCM, and spontaneous worship in a way that resonates with millions worldwide. Since their inception, they have amassed billions of streams across platforms, topping Billboard's Christian and Gospel charts with albums like Jubilee and Maverick City, Vol. 3. Their collaborations with renowned artists such as Kirk Franklin, Chandler Moore, and Naomi Raine have cemented their place as a powerhouse in modern worship. With multiple Grammy, Dove, and Stellar Awards, their impact extends beyond music, fostering a movement that unites diverse voices in worship.
